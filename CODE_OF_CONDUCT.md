# Code of Conduct

## Expected conduct
- Be direct, professional, and constructive.
- Focus criticism on the work, not the person.
- Keep issues and pull requests on topic.
- Respect maintainer decisions on scope, public-safety boundaries, and repository standards.

## Unacceptable conduct
- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or abusive language
- Deliberate disruption, spam, or bad-faith reporting
- Posting private, proprietary, or otherwise unsafe material into the public repository

## Enforcement
Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that breaks these rules. Repeated or serious violations may lead to restriction from further participation.

## Reporting
For conduct issues, contact the repository maintainers through GitHub. For security issues, use the private path described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
