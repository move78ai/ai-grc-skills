# AI System Inventory and Shadow AI Discovery

## What this skill does
Workflow-first inventory support for sanctioned and unsanctioned AI systems, ownership mapping, dependency capture, and risk tiering.

## Who it is for
- Teams building or refreshing an enterprise AI inventory.
- Operators chasing shadow AI usage and unknown ownership gaps.
- Reviewers who need inventory data that can feed compliance, security, and supplier workflows.

## Use this skill when
- You need an AI inventory or registry design.
- You need shadow AI discovery and remediation planning.
- You need ownership mapping, classification, or risk-tiering support.

## Typical inputs
- Known system lists, discovery sources, business owners, and usage signals.
- Third-party dependencies, data flows, and current governance records.
- Risk criteria, exception history, or attestation expectations.

## Typical outputs
- Inventory register or discovery log.
- Owner matrix, dependency record, or risk-tiering worksheet.
- Remediation backlog, attestation plan, or executive summary.

## How it works
- Combine sanctioned inventory data with discovery signals that expose unsanctioned use.
- Separate classification, ownership, dependency, and remediation decisions.
- Use the register as a living control artifact, not a one-time snapshot.
