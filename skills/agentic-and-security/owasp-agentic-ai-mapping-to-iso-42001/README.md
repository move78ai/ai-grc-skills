# OWASP Agentic AI Mapping to ISO/IEC 42001

## What this skill does
Workflow-first mapping support that turns OWASP Agentic AI findings into ISO/IEC 42001 control, evidence, and remediation views.

## Who it is for
- Teams converting OWASP Agentic AI findings into governance and assurance actions.
- Operators who need evidence requests, mapped controls, or residual-risk decisions.
- Reviewers who want a clean bridge between security findings and management-system actions.

## Use this skill when
- You need to map OWASP Agentic AI Top 10 findings to ISO/IEC 42001.
- You need an agentic-security gap view expressed as control and evidence work.
- You need executive, audit, or supplier-facing outputs tied to the mapping.

## Typical inputs
- Use case description, identified findings, and current control posture.
- Relevant clauses, Annex A controls, or audit expectations already in scope.
- Known evidence gaps, suppliers, or release decisions tied to the findings.

## Typical outputs
- Crosswalk tracker and evidence request pack.
- Implementation action register or residual-risk memo.
- Executive or audit summary of mapped control implications.

## How it works
- Treat each OWASP finding as an implementation signal, not as a standalone conclusion.
- Map findings into control themes, evidence needs, and owner actions.
- Carry forward the unresolved items into approval or remediation decisions.
