# AI Board Reporting / Executive Oversight

## What this skill does
Workflow-first executive-reporting support for board packs, dashboards, top-risk views, and residual-risk decisions.

## Who it is for
- Teams translating AI governance and risk data into board-ready material.
- Operators preparing executive dashboards, heatmaps, or top-risk summaries.
- Reviewers who need clean residual-risk, exception, or incident briefing materials.

## Use this skill when
- You need a board pack, executive dashboard, or portfolio heatmap.
- You need a residual-risk memo, waiver, or decision request for leadership.
- You need incident, supplier, or regulatory exposure summarized for senior reviewers.

## Typical inputs
- Portfolio risks, incidents, supplier issues, assurance status, and pending decisions.
- Key metrics, trend data, open actions, and accountabilities.
- Audience, meeting context, and level of detail expected by leadership.

## Typical outputs
- Board pack, dashboard, or heatmap.
- Top-risk register, decision memo, or management action tracker.
- Incident or regulatory exposure briefing tailored for executives.

## How it works
- Strip the detail down to what a board or executive committee must decide or monitor.
- Tie each risk or issue to owners, deadlines, and requested decisions.
- Keep portfolio view, residual risk, and management action status visible together.
